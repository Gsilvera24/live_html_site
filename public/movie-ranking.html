The Best Movies According to Gerardo

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Matrix

A revolutionary movie about the concept of reality. Could reality be a simulation? or are we just living in real time.

A.I Artificial Intelligence

One of Steven Spielbergs best works. It tells the story about and Android named David who lives in the future and develops a sentient ability.

Interstellar

Super beautiful film. Really artistic about space travel
